Governance Domains

COR0110268.11P supports governance across multiple domains, each with specialized modules and workflows.

  • Financial Governance - Regulatory compliance and risk management for financial institutions. Covers banking regulations, anti-money laundering (AML), know-your-customer (KYC), and financial reporting requirements. Includes automated compliance monitoring and risk assessment for financial transactions.
  • Healthcare Governance - HIPAA compliance and patient data governance. Manages protected health information (PHI), patient privacy rights, and healthcare regulatory requirements. Provides secure data handling, audit trails for medical decisions, and compliance with healthcare standards.
  • Supply Chain Governance - Vendor management and logistics compliance. Oversees supplier relationships, contract compliance, and supply chain risk management. Includes automated vendor assessments, performance monitoring, and regulatory compliance for global supply chains.
  • IT Security Governance - Cybersecurity frameworks and incident response. Implements security policies, vulnerability management, and incident response procedures. Provides continuous monitoring, threat detection, and compliance with security standards like ISO 27001 and NIST.
  • Environmental Governance - Sustainability reporting and regulatory compliance. Manages environmental impact assessments, emissions tracking, and sustainability reporting. Supports compliance with environmental regulations and corporate social responsibility initiatives.
